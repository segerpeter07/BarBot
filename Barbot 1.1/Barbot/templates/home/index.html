Drinks Tracking

Users sync their accounts with a wrist band barcode at the party and are able to track their drinkns then on. Each drink after is added to their account and the users can go back after the party and when they got a drink in a beautiful graphical display.

Party Captain Suite

Party hosts have access to a full suite of logistics tools including stock tracking so they know exactly when to resupply the bar. They also have access to tools to track users based off their BAC so they can ensure their party is of the utmost safety standards

Data Visualization

Both users and hosts have access to a broad range of data visualization tools that allow them to keep an eye on the party before, during, and after. Additional tools help the hosts ensure they are staying on budget, as well a help forcast earnings for the night
